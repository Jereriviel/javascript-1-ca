About Us

RainyDays is dedicated to providing exceptional gear for those who refuse to let the weather dictate their adventures. Our commitment to quality, community, and sustainability makes us more than just an apparel brand—we are a partner in your outdoor pursuits.

Our History

Founded in the north of Norway, RainyDays emerged from a group of enthusiastic outdoor adventurers who were determined to confront the region's unpredictable weather with gear that could stand the test of any climate. Established in 2011, RainyDays began with a single mission: to craft the finest waterproof and durable sport jackets that cater to the needs of the avid explorer, regardless of the season or weather condition.

The founders, a trio of lifelong friends and avid hikers, noticed a gap in the market for high-quality, versatile outdoor attire that could seamlessly transition from a casual hike to a rigorous skiing trip. They pooled their savings, invested in premium materials, and collaborated with experts in textile technology to develop their first line of jackets. The result was a range of products that not only provided exceptional protection from the elements but also maintained comfort and style—qualities that quickly made RainyDays a beloved brand among outdoor enthusiasts.

As the company grew, so did its range of offerings. Today, RainyDays is a beacon for outdoor apparel, providing a robust catalogue of jackets that are celebrated for their craftsmanship, innovation, and ability to empower adventurers to explore the great outdoors in comfort and style.

Our Values

At RainyDays, our core values are woven into every thread of our jackets and are reflected in every facet of our business. We believe in sustainability, integrity, and community—principles that guide our operations and our interactions with customers, employees, and the environment.

Sustainability

We are committed to reducing our environmental impact. RainyDays uses recycled materials in our jackets and packaging, and we continually strive to improve our manufacturing processes to conserve natural resources and reduce waste.

Integrity

We stand by the quality of our products with a guarantee of durability. Our jackets are designed to endure, and we ensure that every item meets stringent standards of quality before it reaches our customers. Transparency in our materials sourcing and production methods is paramount to build trust and uphold the high standards we are known for.

Community

RainyDays is more than just a company; we are a community of passionate individuals who share a love for the outdoors. We engage with our customers through workshops, sponsored events, and environmental conservation efforts to foster a sense of belonging and collective care for our planet.

Innovation

Driven by a passion for continuous improvement and technological advancement, we embrace innovation to enhance functionality and performance in all our products. Our team stays on the cutting edge of material science and design trends to ensure our gear meets the evolving needs of our adventurers.
